About Kay Birks

Bringing you the finest Birkenstock collection with premium comfort and timeless style

Our Story

Kay Birks Shop was founded by Rich Software Engineer Christian Kwabena Asante with a passion for bringing the authentic German craftsmanship of Birkenstock to comfort enthusiasts All across Ghana . We believe that every step should be a comfortable one, and every slipper should tell a story of quality and tradition.

With beginnings that date back to 2024 November , Kay Birks has a deep history and heritage embedded within each design made. Set apart by traditional European craftsmanship, the cork and latex footbed imitates a natural footprint in the sand for a personalized fit.
